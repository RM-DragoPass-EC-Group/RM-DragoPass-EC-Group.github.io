Welcome to the Electronic-Control Group of the DragoPass RoboMaster Team!

Welcome to the Electronic-Control Group of the DragoPass RoboMaster Team! We specialize in developing advanced embedded controllers, optimizing control algorithms, and creating robust electronic hardware for our competition robots. Our mission is to push the boundaries of robotics through innovation and teamwork, ensuring our robots are powerful, intelligent, and efficient. Join us as we lead the charge in robotics excellence, combining technical expertise with a passion for innovation.

Explore our projects, meet our talented team members, and stay updated with our latest achievements. Welcome to the heart of DragoPass – where technology and creativity meet to drive the future of robotics.
